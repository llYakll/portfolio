Professional Life


I am currently a telecommunications contractor for Verizon Wireless, U.S. Cellular, AT&T, and am responsible for the maintenance and upkeep of 36 Missouri county e-911 systems. I have done a little bit of everything communications related the in the past 10 years, from building the structure, mounting equipment, or simply changing a light bulb! This industry has made me accustomed to long work days and a harsh working environment which is why i'm seeking a change in my career path. As you can imagine, hanging equipment at an elevation of several hundred feet in all varieties of weather conditions takes it's toll on the body. While I have been well compensated for my knowledge and physical ability in this field, age has determined it's time to move in another direction.
